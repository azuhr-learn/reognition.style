Recognition Style Quiz

Instructions: This tool helps you discover your preferred recognition style at work. Read each group of statements and rank the five items in each group from 1 (Least like me) to 5 (Most like me). Use each number only once per group.

Group A – What makes me feel most recognised at work?
Group B – What motivates me at work?
Group C – What feels least like recognition to me?
Group D – What kind of appreciation is most meaningful?
